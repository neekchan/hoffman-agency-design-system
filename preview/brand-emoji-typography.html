Emoji typography

Let an emoji 🗣️ a word.

A signature voice move: swap one charged word for an emoji that carries it. It reads as wit, not decoration. Keep it to the few words that earn it.

We turn opinion into 📈 influence.

📈 stands in for rising — the word the reader pictures anyway.

Counsel without conviction is just 🤷.

🤷 carries indecision — the line still reads if you swap the word back in.

Clarity ⬇️ · complexity ⬆️

Works on navy too — the glyph keeps its own color, the type stays brand.
Do
  • Replace one charged noun or verb — the word a reader pictures anyway.
  • Wrap it in .tha-emoji so it rides the baseline, not above the line.
  • Always add role="img" aria-label="…" with the word it replaces.
Don't
  • More than 3 per surface (voice rule) — past that it's clip-art.
  • Emoji as bullets, icons, or decoration. This is in place of a word only.
  • Skin-tone or brand-logo emoji; keep them neutral and evergreen.
REMEMBER · the sentence must still read perfectly if the emoji were the word it replaces
