The rule
Do — add class="tha-theme-white" to a section to flip it to the white model. Cards inside automatically become sand. Both models keep a hairline --border-2 so panels read even at low contrast.
Don't — put a paper card on a paper page, or a white card on a white page. The ~1.5% delta is too faint to read as elevation — the washed, flat look to avoid.
