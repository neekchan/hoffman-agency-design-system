7th technique: Highlights — a CSS marker-pen overlay over body text. Use any brand color as the highlight background, with a WCAG-safe text/highlight contrast pair (e.g. navy text on lime, white on purple, navy on cyan, paper on teal). Counts within the 1–2 marks per surface budget.

Rules: 1–2 marks per surface. Color is contextual — opposite/contrasting the surface so the mark reads clearly. Stay within the brand palette (lime, lavender, purple, cyan, teal, navy, paper). The Storyline squiggle has its own two-uses rule and is not part of this set.
