Which pairs you can actually read.

Row = background, column = text color. Each cell shows the contrast ratio and its highest WCAG pass. Use this to unlock more brand combinations safely — and to see why lime-on-white never works as text.

Thresholds: AAA ≥ 7.0 · AA (body text) ≥ 4.5 · AA Large (≥ 24px or 19px bold, and UI/graphics) ≥ 3.0 · below 3.0 fails. Ratios are symmetric, so the table is mirrored across the diagonal.
